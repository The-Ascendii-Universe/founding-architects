## ğŸ§  Core Principles

These principles guide every decision, from technical architecture to governance design:

### **Digital Sovereignty**  
Users own their identity, data, assets, and contributions. No platform can take away what you've built or lock you into a walled garden.

### **Interoperability First**  
Systems are designed to work *with* other platforms and protocols, not against them. We build bridges, not walls.

### **Transparency by Default**  
Governance processes, decision-making rationale, and resource allocation are visible and auditable. Trust through verification, not obfuscation.

### **Human-Centered Systems**  
Technology serves people, not the other way around. Metrics of success include human flourishing, not just growth and engagement.

### **Co-Creation Over Extraction**  
Value is grown together and shared equitably. Contributors are stakeholders, not resources to be optimized.

### **Long-Term Over Urgency**  
We optimize for what will matter in 10 years, not what will trend in 10 days. Patience and intentionality over "move fast and break things."

### **Stewardship Over Ownership**  
We are caretakers of something larger than ourselves, building for those who come after us.
